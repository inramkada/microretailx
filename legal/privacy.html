Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how MICRORETAILX LLC (“MICRORETAILX”, “we”, “us”, “our”) may process personal data in connection with this Website. The Website is engineered under a Zero Data Strategy by default: no accounts are required, no behavioural profiling is performed by default, and optional analytics/marketing technologies are disabled unless and until you provide explicit consent where required.

This Policy is intended to operate globally and to respect applicable privacy and ePrivacy requirements wherever the Website is accessed. Nothing in this Policy limits any mandatory rights you may have under applicable law.

Zero Data Strategy (Default)

The Website is designed to minimise personal data processing. In practical terms:

  • No accounts are required to access core Website content.
  • No visitor profiling is performed by default.
  • No third-party tracking is activated by default.
  • Optional analytics/marketing technologies are loaded only after explicit, granular user consent where required.

Data we may process (limited technical data)

Like most websites, limited technical information may be processed to deliver the Website, maintain security, ensure integrity, and prevent abuse. This may include, depending on infrastructure and network conditions: IP address, user-agent string, request timestamps, requested URL, referrer headers, and security signals used for rate-limiting, bot mitigation, or troubleshooting.

This technical processing is used for security, availability, integrity, and delivery purposes and is not used for behavioural advertising or selling personal data.

Cookies and consent

The Website may use strictly necessary cookies (or similar technologies) required to deliver core functionality, maintain security, and store consent choices. Optional cookies (analytics/marketing) are disabled by default and are only enabled after consent where required. See the Cookie Policy for additional detail.

Consent preferences are stored locally in your browser. A locally generated reference code (referred to as a CONSENT REF) may be created on your device to reference your current consent state on that specific browser or device. The CONSENT REF is not intended to identify you personally or track you across websites.

Strict security posture (policy and practice)

We maintain strict security policies and technical safeguards designed to protect the Website and any limited data processed for security and delivery. Our approach is based on prevention, detection, and rapid remediation.

  • Secure-by-default configuration: HTTPS enforcement, modern TLS, and hardened security headers where supported.
  • Access control: least-privilege administrative access and protected operational workflows.
  • Change control: controlled updates, reviews, and rollback capability for production-facing changes.
  • Abuse prevention: rate limiting, bot mitigation, and security filtering through delivery/security layers.
  • Incident readiness: monitoring signals, triage, and remediation workflows designed to reduce exposure and downtime.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the Website, taking into account the nature of the Website and the limited data processed. Any references to security practices are descriptive only and do not constitute a guarantee.

Infrastructure providers (hosting / CDN / security layers)

The Website may be hosted and delivered via third-party infrastructure providers (including static hosting and delivery/security layers). These providers may perform caching, traffic routing, rate limiting, and security filtering to maintain availability and protect against malicious traffic.

Such providers may process limited technical data for delivery and security. We configure the Website to minimise exposure and do not enable optional tracking by default.

Retention

We retain personal data (if any) only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy (security, integrity, delivery, troubleshooting, and abuse prevention), unless longer retention is required by law. Infrastructure providers may apply their own retention windows.

International processing and transfers

The Website is globally accessible and may involve infrastructure in multiple jurisdictions. Where applicable law requires transfer safeguards, we may implement appropriate measures consistent with legal requirements.
