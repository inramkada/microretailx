1. Purpose and Scope

1.1 This Privacy Policy explains how MICRORETAILX LLC (“MICRORETAILX”, “we”, “us”, “our”) processes personal data in connection with this Website and related communications.

1.2 The Website follows a strict Zero Data Strategy. Its function is to provide information. MICRORETAILX does not seek to collect, analyze, profile, monetize, or commercially exploit visitor data.

1.3 This Policy is drafted under a GDPR-first approach and is intended to comply with the EU GDPR, UK GDPR, and applicable ePrivacy rules. It also aims to align with generally accepted data protection principles applicable in Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, and the Americas.

2. Controller and Contact

2.1 Data controller: MICRORETAILX LLC, a company incorporated in the State of Delaware, United States.

2.2 Privacy and legal contact: legal@microretailx.com.

3. DPO / EU Representative

3.1 A Data Protection Officer (DPO) has not been appointed, as the current processing activities do not meet the legal thresholds requiring such designation.

3.2 An EU or UK representative pursuant to Article 27 GDPR / UK GDPR has not been designated, as the Website is informational in nature and does not involve targeted or large-scale regulated processing. Where legally required, representative details will be published in this Policy.

4. Categories of Personal Data We Process

4.1 Strictly necessary technical and security data processed automatically to deliver and protect the Website. This may include limited request metadata, timestamps, requested resources, basic device or browser headers, and security or abuse-prevention signals.

4.2 Cookie preference data stored locally on your device to remember and enforce your consent choices.

4.3 Communications data voluntarily provided by you when contacting us (for example, via email, or via a contact form if enabled in the future), limited strictly to the information you choose to provide.

4.4 MICRORETAILX does not knowingly collect sensitive personal data, does not use marketing or advertising cookies, and does not engage in behavioural tracking.

5. Purposes

5.1 To provide access to Website content and information.

5.2 To ensure network, information, and operational security, including prevention of abuse and malicious activity.

5.3 To store and respect cookie preference selections on your device.

5.4 To respond to inquiries and communications initiated by you.

6. Legal Bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

6.1 Website delivery and security are processed on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) in operating a secure and reliable website, and where applicable, the performance of a service requested by the user (Article 6(1)(b)).

6.2 Strictly necessary cookies are processed based on necessity for service provision and/or legitimate interests, in accordance with applicable ePrivacy laws.

6.3 Optional analytics, if ever enabled, would rely exclusively on your prior consent (Article 6(1)(a)).

6.4 Communications are processed based on legitimate interests, pre-contractual steps where applicable, and legal obligations.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies (ePrivacy)

7.1 The Website uses strictly necessary cookies to operate correctly and to store cookie preference selections.

7.2 Marketing cookies are not used.

7.3 Optional analytics remain disabled by default and would only be activated following explicit opt-in by you.

8. Consent Reference Hash (Browser-Side Accountability)

8.1 To support accountability while minimizing data, the Website may generate a one-way cryptographic hash (SHA-256) of your cookie preference record locally within your browser.

8.2 This reference is stored exclusively on your device (for example, via local storage or a cookie).

8.3 MICRORETAILX does not receive, store, or process this browser-side consent reference on its servers unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, within an email to support).

9. Processors and Service Providers

9.1 MICRORETAILX uses Cloudflare as a global security and content delivery provider. Cloudflare may process limited technical data as part of providing network security, performance optimization, and abuse prevention.

9.2 For communications, MICRORETAILX uses privacy-focused encrypted email services (including Proton). End-to-end encryption is supported where technically available and configured by the communicating parties.

10. Sharing and Disclosure

10.1 MICRORETAILX does not sell personal data.

10.2 Personal data may be disclosed only where required by applicable law, court order, or competent authority, or where necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.

11. International Transfers

11.1 MICRORETAILX LLC is established in the United States and operates globally, including across Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, and the Americas.

11.2 Where personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland is transferred internationally, appropriate safeguards are implemented where required by law.

12. Retention

12.1 Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy and to comply with applicable legal obligations.

12.2 Cookie preferences and browser-side consent references remain stored on your device.

12.3 Communications are retained for a reasonable period to manage responses and compliance, after which they are deleted or minimized.

13. Security

13.1 Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect the Website and communications, taking into account the nature of the data and the risks involved.

14. Your Rights (EEA / UK / CH)

14.1 Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing, and to withdraw consent where applicable.

14.2 Requests may be submitted to legal@microretailx.com. Proportionate identity verification may be required.

14.3 You may lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

15. Automated Decision-Making

15.1 MICRORETAILX does not engage in automated decision-making or profiling producing legal or similarly significant effects.

16. Children

16.1 This Website is not intended for children under the age of 16, and MICRORETAILX does not knowingly collect children’s personal data.

17. Changes

17.1 This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the current version.
