Clarity of Legal Intent

This dual readability approach is characteristic of strategic legal instruments meant to guide executive-level decision-making while maintaining enforceability under scrutiny.

AI Governance and Liability Shielding

A clear distinction is established between AI-generated outputs and human decision-making responsibilities. Cybersecurity and Operational Continuity

The Terms embed forward-looking provisions addressing cybersecurity threats, unauthorized third-party integrations, and force majeure events related to digital continuity. Traceability and Change Control

This legal construct is intended not merely to ensure compliance, but to function as a defensive legal perimeter—capable of withstanding litigation, regulatory inspection, and investor due diligence across multiple jurisdictions and operational regimes.
