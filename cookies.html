Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how MICRORETAILX LLC ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or interact with our platform or associated domains (the "System").

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us operate the System, personalize content, remember your preferences, analyze usage, and serve advertisements.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies – Required for the System to function properly. Cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies – Enable enhanced features like remembering settings or preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies – Collect usage and performance data (e.g., page views, errors).
  • Marketing Cookies – Track browsing activity to deliver personalized ads across services.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process cookies based on the legal grounds of:

  • Consent – For non-essential cookies, including analytics and marketing.
  • Legitimate Interest – For essential cookies strictly required for system security or availability.

4. Consent Management

Upon your first visit, you will be presented with a cookie banner allowing you to accept, reject, or customize your cookie preferences. You may update your choices anytime by clicking “Cookie Settings” in the footer.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow select partners (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Cloudflare) to set cookies on your device. These third parties process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Retention Periods

Cookies are retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill their purpose, with automatic expiration ranging from a single session to up to 24 months, depending on the type.

7. How to Control Cookies

  • Via our on-site “Cookie Settings” panel
  • Through your browser settings (e.g., block, delete or limit cookies)
  • Opt-out from targeted advertising via YourOnlineChoices.eu or optout.aboutads.info

8. International Compliance

This Policy is aligned with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or law. All updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date".
